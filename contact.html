FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us!

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies based on project complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while complex e-commerce or custom platforms may take 4-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

What is the cost of website development?

Our pricing starts from ₹25,000 for basic websites and goes up based on features and complexity. We offer flexible packages and custom pricing. Contact us for a free, detailed quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! All our packages include post-launch support ranging from 3-12 months. We also offer ongoing maintenance plans to ensure your website stays updated, secure, and performing optimally.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we create are fully responsive and optimized for all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile-first design is our standard approach.

Do you offer SEO services?

Yes! We provide comprehensive SEO services including on-page optimization, technical SEO, keyword research, and link building. Basic SEO is included in all our website packages.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes, we specialize in website redesigns! We can modernize your existing site while preserving your content and SEO rankings. We'll analyze your current site and provide recommendations for improvement.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, UPI, credit/debit cards, and digital wallets. Payment is typically split into milestones: 50% upfront and 50% upon project completion.

Do I own the website after completion?

Yes! Upon final payment, you receive complete ownership of the website, including all source code, designs, and content. We provide all necessary credentials and documentation.
